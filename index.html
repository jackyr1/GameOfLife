Game of Life

Welcome to the University of California Santa Cruz!

This website is designed for users to understand and navigate UCSC better! In the beginning, you will make a very important decision that leads to more information given to you. We hope that with this website new or aspiring students can understand this school and its benefits since often time official websites can be overwhelming and intimidating. This website was made by UCSC students who have all been through the nerves of going to college and navigating a new school!

Heres how to play!

At the beginning you will make an important decision, after that to continue playing click the 'Next' button. If you want to go back to the beginning just click the 'Back' button! Below is a map of the school, after taking a look click the 'START!' button to get going!
